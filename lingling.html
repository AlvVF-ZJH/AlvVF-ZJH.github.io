Contemporary cultural IP inspired by Lingnan heritage and traditional symbolism.

OVERVIEW

Ling Ling is a cultural character IP project inspired by Lingnan traditions, architecture, and regional symbolism. The project reinterprets traditional cultural elements through contemporary illustration and motion design.

Brand Identity Illustration Motion Design Cultural Design

VISUAL DEVELOPMENT

The character system combines traditional motifs, warm color palettes, and simplified geometric forms to create a playful but culturally grounded visual identity.

ANIMATION

A short animated narrative was developed using After Effects and Premiere Pro, bringing the character and surrounding world into motion through layered transitions and environmental storytelling.

OUTCOME

The project received Bronze Overall and #1 Judges’ Choice among over 100 nationwide competition entries.
