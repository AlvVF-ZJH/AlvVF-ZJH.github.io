NUMI

Mental health platform translating psychological exercises into interactive daily rituals.

Overview

NUMI is a digital mental health platform where users care for a symbolic rice grain representing emotional growth. The project translates psychological principles into interactive daily rituals through responsive UI experiences.

Process

The platform was designed in Figma, from wireframes to high-fidelity prototypes. A modular UI system was developed to ensure consistency across web and mobile experiences.

Outcome

The final system improved workflow efficiency and reduced design iteration time by 30%.
